YOASOBI

「ラブレター」/loveLetter

YOASOBI is a Japanese music duo that was formed and debuted in 2019 under Sony Music Entertainment Japan

The duo debuted with the single "Yoru ni Kakeru" in December 2019. It became successful on the music chart in Japan with charting atop for three consecutive weeks and 2020 year-end on Billboard Japan Hot 100. Their debut extended play The Book was released in January 2021 and reached number two on both Oricon Albums Chart and Billboard Japan Hot Albums.
